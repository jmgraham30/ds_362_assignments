Problem 5

Suppose that \(\| \cdot \|\) is a vector norm and let \({\bf u}\) and \({\bf v}\) be vectors. Show that

\[ | \|u\| - \|v\| | \leq \| u - v \| \]

Hint: This can be done by noting that \(\|{\bf u}\| = \|{\bf u} - {\bf v} + {\bf v} \|\) and then manipulating the triangle inequality.
